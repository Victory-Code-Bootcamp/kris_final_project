Who We Are

We are a community of peers who have lived experience in areas of homelessness, incarceration, mental health problems and substance abuse. We've been there before and know that you don't have to feel hopeless forever. If you don't want to do it alone, you don't have to do so.

What We Do

Homes for the Homeless is a local non-profit organization that specializes in matching people who live outside with temporary and permanent housing solutions.
